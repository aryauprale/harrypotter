Hogwarts Houses

Hogwarts houses are four groups at the wizarding school, each representing different values and traits: Gryffindor (bravery), Hufflepuff (loyalty), Ravenclaw (intelligence), and Slytherin (ambition).

Gryffindor: Founded by Godric Gryffindor, this house values bravery, courage, and chivalry. Its emblematic animal is the lion, and its colors are scarlet and gold.
Hufflepuff: Founded by Helga Hufflepuff, this house is known for its values of hard work, loyalty, and fairness. The badger is its emblematic animal, and its colors are yellow and black.
Ravenclaw: Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw, this house emphasizes intelligence, creativity, and a love of learning. The eagle represents Ravenclaw, and its colors are blue and bronze. Famous members include Luna Lovegood and Cho Chang.
Slytherin: Founded by Salazar Slytherin, this house values ambition, cunning, and resourcefulness. The serpent is its emblem, and its colors are green and silver. Notable members include Draco Malfoy and Severus Snape
