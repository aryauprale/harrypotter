Characters List

Here is a comprehensive list of almost all the characters within Harry Potter

Main Trio
Harry Potter: The protagonist, "The Boy Who Lived." Hermione Granger: Harry's intelligent and resourceful friend. Ron Weasley: Harry's loyal best friend.
Hogwarts Staff
Albus Dumbledore: Headmaster of Hogwarts. Minerva McGonagall: Transfiguration professor and Deputy Headmistress. Severus Snape: Potions Master and later Headmaster. Rubeus Hagrid: Keeper of Keys and Grounds; later Care of Magical Creatures professor. Gilderoy Lockhart: Defense Against the Dark Arts professor (Year 2). Remus Lupin: Defense Against the Dark Arts professor (Year 3). Dolores Umbridge: Defense Against the Dark Arts professor (Year 5). Filius Flitwick: Charms professor. Pomona Sprout: Herbology professor. Sybill Trelawney: Divination professor. Horace Slughorn: Potions Master (Years 6 & 7).
The Weasley Family
Arthur Weasley: Patriarch of the Weasley family, works at the Ministry of Magic. Molly Weasley: Matriarch, known for her warmth and fierce protection of her family. Bill Weasley: Curse-breaker for Gringotts. Charlie Weasley: Works with dragons in Romania. Percy Weasley: Ambitious, works at the Ministry. Fred and George Weasley: Mischievous twins, founders of Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes. Ginny Weasley: Ron’s younger sister, later Harry’s love interest.
The Order of the Phoenix
Sirius Black: Harry’s godfather. Lupin: (also part of the Order). Mad-Eye Moody: Auror, known for his vigilance. Nymphadora Tonks: Young Auror, known for her Metamorphmagus abilities. Kingsley Shacklebolt: Auror, later Minister for Magic. Aberforth Dumbledore: Albus’s brother.
The Death Eaters
Voldemort (Tom Riddle): The main antagonist. Bellatrix Lestrange: Loyal Death Eater. Lucius Malfoy: Wealthy Death Eater and Draco’s father. Narcissa Malfoy: Draco’s mother, not a Death Eater but loyal to her family. Peter Pettigrew: Betrayer of Harry’s parents. Fenrir Greyback: Werewolf allied with Voldemort.
Hogwarts Students
Draco Malfoy: Harry’s rival at school. Neville Longbottom: Loyal friend of Harry, later hero in the battle against Voldemort. Luna Lovegood: Eccentric Ravenclaw student and Harry’s friend. Cedric Diggory: Hufflepuff student and Triwizard champion. Cho Chang: Harry’s first crush. Dean Thomas: Gryffindor student. Seamus Finnigan: Harry’s Gryffindor classmate. Lavender Brown: Gryffindor student, briefly Ron’s girlfriend. Parvati Patil: Gryffindor student, attended Yule Ball with Harry.
Magical Creatures
Dobby: House-elf loyal to Harry. Kreacher: House-elf of the Black family. Hedwig: Harry’s snowy owl. Buckbeak: Hippogriff owned by Hagrid. Fawkes: Dumbledore’s phoenix. Other Notable Characters Lily and James Potter: Harry’s parents, killed by Voldemort. Vernon, Petunia, and Dudley Dursley: Harry’s unpleasant Muggle relatives. Rita Skeeter: Journalist for the Daily Prophet. Argus Filch: Hogwarts caretaker. Moaning Myrtle: Ghost in the girls’ bathroom. Nearly Headless Nick: Ghost of Gryffindor House

  

Main Characters In Harry Potter

These are a few of the characters that are important to the story.

Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger is one of Harry Potter's closest friends, known for her intelligence, resourcefulness, and strong work ethic. She is an ambitious young woman that fights for the rights of house elves and against evil forces.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter,famous before he even knows magic, is a brave and loyal young wizard who rises from an orphaned childhood to become a key figure in the fight against dark forces for freedom.
